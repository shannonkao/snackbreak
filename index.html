Mixtape Cassette Zine (2023)

The Mixtape Cassette Zine is a collection of fifteen accordion zines by different artists. Every participant created a playlist, then designed a small (cassette-sized!) zine version of the playlist, with each page corresponding to a song.

I Spy Zine (2022)

A collaboration between six artists, with each artist creating an illustrative I Spy spread for this sixteen-page booklet. The back cover provides a checklist of items across all illustrations, and the cover is a metapuzzle of bonus objects to be found in the book.
