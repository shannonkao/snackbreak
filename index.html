projects about vivian shannon

snack break studio is a small collective based out of Seattle, WA. We make comics, illustrations and zines of all flavors!

We have a risograph, handheld perforator, two metal rulers, sewing machine, and lots of embroidery needles :)

Mixtape Cassette Zine (2023)

The Mixtape Cassette Zine is a collection of fifteen accordion zines by different artists packaged in a clear cassette case. Every participant created a playlist, then designed a 4"x2.75" zine version of the playlist, with each page corresponding to a song.

ABC Cooking (2023)

ABC Cooking is five recipes of Chinese home-cooking recorded over 24-pages. Its cover is a two color silkscreen print.

sky/sea (2023)

An 8-page booklet alternating blue and white pages. Each page is a solid color and uses the color of the page behind it to create the impression of a seascape. Risograph printed, hand-cut, hand- bound.

Flower Patrol (2023)

A collection of three mini mini zines with one bookmark of Flower Patrol glaring at a random character. Each zine is an installation of Flower Patrol trying to govern over creatures it has no control over.

I Spy Zine (2022)

A collaboration between six artists, with each artist creating an illustrative I Spy spread for this sixteen-page booklet. The back cover provides a checklist of items across all illustrations, and the cover is a metapuzzle of bonus objects to be found in the book.

You Can't Get There From Here (2020)

Two sixteen-page zines each containing unique diptychs of strange, overgrown, and forgotten places.

Vivian is a comics artist, illustrator, basketball pro, and expert chef currently living in Seattle. She can be found wearing the color yellow, often in or near the water (very like a duck), equipped with watercolors and a camera (not like a duck). Vivian tells funny, charming, and kind stories about occurrences both everyday and fantastic.

Vivian likes fruits and hates carrots.
